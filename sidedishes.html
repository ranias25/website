Asian Cucumber Salad

Ingredients:

  • 6 mini cucumbers or Persian cucumbers
  • 2 teaspoon salt (to drain water)
  • 1 tablespoon regular soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar or white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon chinese chili oil
  • 1 tablespoon white granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds
  • 2 tablespoon green onion
  1. Wash cucumbers and cut off the ends.
  2. Place a cucumber between two chopsticks. Make thin diagonal slives on the top, then flip it and do the same to the other side, but make sure for it to not be at a straight angle.
  3. Cut the cucumbers in half and put them in a bowl.
  4. Sprinkle salt over cucumbers and massage them. Let them soak for 5-10 minutes, and then rinse with cold water 3-4 times. Strain and set aside.
  5. Combine the dressing ingredients in a seperate bowl.
  6. Pour the dressing over them and mix gently. Consume
